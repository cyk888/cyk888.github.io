<h1>
Introduction to Yunlong Lake
</h1>
<i>
Yunlong Lake is located in the southwest of Xuzhou City, Jiangsu Province. It is the main scenic spot of Yunlong Lake scenic spot in Xuzhou, formerly known as "Shigou Lake", which was formed in the Northern Song Dynasty at the latest. <br>Yunlong Lake is adjacent to Yunlong mountain in the East, Han mountain and Tianqi mountain in the West. Nestled in the south by spring mountain and Zhushan mountain. Surrounded by mountains on three sides, facing the city on one side.            The original water surface of Yunlong Lake is 5.8 square kilometers, and the land area is 5.6 square kilometers.. <br>
Taking Huzhong road as the boundary, the perimeter of East Lake is about 8.1km, that of West Lake is about 7km, and that of the whole lake is about 12km. <br>
If shangxiaonanhu scenic area is included (the total area is 1.661 square kilometers, including 0.962 square kilometers of water area), Yunlong Lake has reached 6.76 square kilometers of water surface area and 6.3 square kilometers of land area.            Yunlong Lake is the best natural landscape in Xuzhou, and it is also one of the landmark scenic spots in Xuzhou. <br>



In August 2016, it was awarded the national AAAAA level scenic spot. 
</i>
